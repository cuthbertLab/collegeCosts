Why Do We Need This?

The U.S. Department of Education has recently released an amazing College Scorecard to see how much various colleges cost and how well they do at graduating students.

There seemed to me to be a few important pieces of information missing, the most important being "How much will this college cost Me?" given me and my family's income and assets (and, for public colleges, which state I live in) and "Show me only colleges I have a realistic chance of being admitted to."
